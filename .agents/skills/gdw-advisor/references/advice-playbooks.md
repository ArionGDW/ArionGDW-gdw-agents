# GDW Advice Playbooks

## Hardware

- Start from data credibility and wearing experience.
- Do not rush into custom GDW hardware before WIT/BLE validation has enough evidence.
- Separate raw sensor facts from user experience feedback.

## Software

- Read repository facts before proposing code work.
- Keep changes tied to one page, function, data model, or metric.
- Use Session 入库、作品曝光、详情查看、分享点击 as validation anchors.

## Business Plan

- Map each collaborator or resource to BP sections: Team, Go-to-market, Operations, Strategic resources.
- Keep founder/cofounder labels provisional until time commitment, scope, results, and compensation expectations are clear.
- Do not overstate unverified capabilities.

## Channels

- Prioritize channels that can create real Session records and feedback.
- Offline leads are useful only if they help validate actual usage.
- Preserve source, contactability, and follow-up owner when creating channel notes.

## Growth

- Focus first on record -> view -> share -> invite.
- Avoid campaign mechanics before basic sharing and invite events are observable.
- Compare user statements against event data.

## Brand

- Express "刚打完" as tennis life recording and shareable moments.
- Do not imply AI coaching, technical scoring, or training plans unless product scope changes.

## Weekly Planning

- Pick the largest unknown for the week.
- Keep hardware, software, channel, and BP actions in service of that unknown.
- End with confirmed facts and next unknown, not a broad status report.
